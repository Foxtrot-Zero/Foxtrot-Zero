***My personal project***: _Medotum_

Medotum is a personal project, conceived and built by a French pharmacist, in a strictly individual capacity. It grew from a simple observation made at the counter: getting reliable health information — or even just managing to ask for it — becomes hard when you travel, live abroad, or struggle to communicate in another language. Yet this access should be made easier, especially for people who are isolated or vulnerable, to allow better care. Medotum is one way to help with that.

It remains an information-support tool, nothing more. It never replaces a consultation or the advice of a healthcare professional. Like any database, it may contain errors, inaccuracies, or incomplete or missing information: always check every piece of information with a healthcare professional before any decision. Its author cannot be held responsible for any use that disregards this warning.
